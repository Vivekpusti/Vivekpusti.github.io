About Me

I’m Vivek Pusti, a Software Engineer at CME Group, Bengaluru, with 2+ years of professional experience in building scalable, high-performance regulatory services and data validation systems. I specialize in backend development with Spring Boot, React-based frontends, and cloud-native solutions on GCP.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science (Intelligent Systems specialization) from MIT School of Engineering, Pune. I’m proficient in Java, Python, C++, React.js, and system design, with hands-on experience across full-stack development, DevOps (CI/CD), and container orchestration using Docker and Kubernetes.

I’ve built real-time dashboards, firm summary portals, and emotion-based music recommendation systems using ML/CNNs. I enjoy solving complex challenges, have solved over 500+ problems on LeetCode, and actively participate in CodeChef contests.

I'm constantly learning and love working at the intersection of backend engineering, cloud infrastructure, and AI-driven solutions. You can view my work on GitHub or connect with me on LinkedIn.
